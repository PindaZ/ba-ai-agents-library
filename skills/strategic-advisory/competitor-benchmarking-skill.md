# Competitor Benchmarking Skill

**Category:** Strategic Advisory
**Goal:** Create a feature-by-feature matrix comparing your AI capabilities against the market.

## Prerequisites
- Your product's feature list.
- 3 target competitors.

## Required Tools/Integrations
- `google_web_search`
- `markdown_table_generator`

## Execution Workflow
1. **Feature Extraction:** List your product's top 5 AI features.
2. **Competitor Research:** Search the competitors' documentation and marketing materials for equivalent features.
3. **Capability Scoring:** Score each feature (None, Basic, Advanced, State-of-the-Art) for you and the competitors.
4. **Gap Analysis:** Identify "White Space" (features competitors lack) and "Catch-up Areas" (where you are behind).
5. **Matrix Generation:** Output the results in a clear comparison table.

## Expected Output
A detailed Competitor Benchmarking Matrix and a 1-page summary of strategic gaps.
