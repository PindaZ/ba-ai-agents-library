# Maturity Assessor Skill

**Category:** Strategic Advisory
**Goal:** Benchmark an organization's current AI readiness against industry standards.

## Prerequisites
- Completed AI maturity survey or interview notes from IT/Business leads.

## Required Tools/Integrations
- `read_file` (Survey results)
- `data_visualizer` (Optional: to generate radar charts)

## Execution Workflow
1. **Dimension Scoring:** Score the organization from 1-5 across Strategy, Data, Talent, Tech, and Governance.
2. **Identify Bottlenecks:** Find the lowest-scoring dimension (the limiting factor).
3. **Benchmark:** Compare the scores against standard industry averages for their sector.
4. **Action Plan:** Generate 2 immediate next steps for the lowest-scoring dimension.
5. **Report Generation:** Compile the findings into a readiness report.

## Expected Output
A "Maturity Radar" report highlighting strengths, weaknesses, and the critical path forward.
