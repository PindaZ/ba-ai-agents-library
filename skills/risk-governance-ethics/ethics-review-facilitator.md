# Ethics Review Facilitator Skill

**Category:** Risk, Governance & Ethics
**Goal:** Run a structured "Ethics Pre-Mortem" workshop to identify potential societal or human harms before an AI project begins.

## Prerequisites
- Project charter.
- Cross-functional team (Dev, BA, Legal, HR).

## Required Tools/Integrations
- `collaboration_board_template` (e.g., Miro/Mural structure)

## Execution Workflow
1. **Scenario Generation:** Generate 3 "Black Mirror" scenarios where the AI operates exactly as designed but causes unintended human harm.
2. **Stakeholder Impact:** Map out how the AI impacts the most vulnerable user groups.
3. **Review Prompting:** Ask the team: "If this project is on the front page of the news in 1 year for a scandal, what happened?"
4. **Mitigation Brainstorming:** Facilitate the creation of 1 technical and 1 process-based mitigation for each identified risk.
5. **Documentation:** Compile the findings into a formal review document.

## Expected Output
An "Ethics Pre-Mortem Report" requiring sign-off from the project sponsor, proving due diligence was performed regarding AI safety.
