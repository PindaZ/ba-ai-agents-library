# Onboarding Sequence Designer Skill

**Category:** Change Management & Adoption
**Goal:** Design an automated email or in-app message sequence to guide users through their first 30 days with the AI tool.

## Prerequisites
- Product map.
- List of "Aha!" moments (when a user realizes the value).

## Required Tools/Integrations
- `email_sequence_generator`

## Execution Workflow
1. **Day 1 (Welcome):** Design an email focusing on login and the single easiest, highest-value action to take.
2. **Day 3 (Check-in):** Design a message addressing common early stumbling blocks.
3. **Day 7 (Advanced Feature):** Introduce a slightly more complex use case or prompt template.
4. **Day 14 (Habit Building):** Share a case study of how another user successfully integrated the tool.
5. **Day 30 (Feedback):** Request a CSAT score or review.

## Expected Output
A complete 30-day communication cadence (copy and trigger rules) to ensure high retention rates.
