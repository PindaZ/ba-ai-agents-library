# Operational Readiness Checker Skill

**Category:** Change Management & Adoption
**Goal:** Verify that all non-technical teams (Support, Legal, Ops) are fully prepared before an AI system is deployed to production.

## Prerequisites
- Project launch date.
- Department sign-off list.

## Required Tools/Integrations
- `checklist_generator`

## Execution Workflow
1. **Support Check:** Verify that the Support/Helpdesk team has access to the FAQ and troubleshooting guide.
2. **Legal Check:** Verify that the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service have been updated.
3. **Training Check:** Verify that at least 80% of target users have completed the basic training module.
4. **Rollback Check:** Verify that the "Kill Switch" process has been documented and tested.
5. **Sign-off Matrix:** Generate a final Go/No-Go matrix requiring physical or digital signatures from department heads.

## Expected Output
A "Launch Readiness Checklist" indicating whether the organization is operationally prepared to flip the switch.
