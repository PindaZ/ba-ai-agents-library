# Empathy Map Generator Skill

**Category:** Change Management & Adoption
**Goal:** Create an empathy map to anticipate user resistance and fears regarding a new AI tool.

## Prerequisites
- Target user personas.
- Basic understanding of their current manual workflows.

## Required Tools/Integrations
- `llm_text_generator`
- `markdown_table_generator`

## Execution Workflow
1. **Persona Ingestion:** Input the profile of the target user (e.g., "Senior Accountant").
2. **"Says & Does" Mapping:** Predict what the user currently says about their workload and what they do daily.
3. **"Thinks & Feels" Mapping:** Identify potential unspoken fears (e.g., "Will this AI replace me?", "I'm not technical enough for this").
4. **Pains & Gains Analysis:** Map out their biggest frustrations (Pains) and how the AI will provide relief (Gains).
5. **Mitigation Strategy:** Suggest 3 specific change management tactics to address the identified fears.

## Expected Output
A structured Empathy Map highlighting the emotional barriers to adoption and strategies to overcome them.
