# Value Realization Auditor Skill

**Category:** Change Management & Adoption
**Goal:** Conduct a post-implementation review (6 months post-launch) to see if the AI project actually delivered the promised ROI.

## Prerequisites
- Original Business Case / ROI Projection document.
- Actual usage data and cloud billing reports.

## Required Tools/Integrations
- `financial_comparator`

## Execution Workflow
1. **Cost Comparison:** Compare the estimated API/Cloud costs against the actual billing data over the last 6 months.
2. **Benefit Comparison:** Compare the projected "Hours Saved" or "Revenue Generated" against actual telemetry data.
3. **Adoption Check:** Check the Daily Active Users (DAU) against the projected adoption rate.
4. **Variance Analysis:** If actual ROI is < projected ROI, determine why (e.g., "API costs were 2x higher than expected" or "Users abandoned the tool").
5. **Recommendation:** Suggest whether to "Scale," "Optimize," or "Sunset" the feature.

## Expected Output
A "Value Realization Audit" presented to the C-Suite detailing the true financial impact of the project.
