# Evangelism Pitch Writer Skill

**Category:** Change Management & Adoption
**Goal:** Write a persuasive, "WIIFM" (What's In It For Me) pitch to convince hesitant stakeholders to adopt the AI tool.

## Prerequisites
- Understanding of the stakeholder's primary KPIs.
- The AI tool's core benefits.

## Required Tools/Integrations
- `copywriting_framework` (e.g., PAS - Problem, Agitation, Solution)

## Execution Workflow
1. **Hook Creation:** Start with a statement acknowledging a massive pain point the stakeholder currently faces.
2. **Agitation:** Briefly highlight the cost (in time or money) of continuing the current manual process.
3. **Solution Introduction:** Introduce the AI tool not as "technology," but as a "team assistant."
4. **Benefit Translation:** Map the AI's technical features directly to the stakeholder's KPIs.
5. **Call to Action:** Ask for a specific, low-friction commitment (e.g., "Join a 15-minute pilot demo").

## Expected Output
A persuasive email or presentation script designed to win over internal skeptics.
