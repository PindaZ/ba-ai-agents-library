# Shadow AI Survey Generator Skill

**Category:** Change Management & Adoption
**Goal:** Create a survey to gently discover what unauthorized AI tools employees are using so IT can provide secure alternatives.

## Prerequisites
- Corporate data policy.
- An anonymous survey platform.

## Required Tools/Integrations
- `survey_question_generator`

## Execution Workflow
1. **Introduction Drafting:** Write a non-punitive intro explaining the goal is to *help* them get the tools they need securely, not punish them.
2. **Usage Questions:** Ask what tools they use (ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney) and for what tasks.
3. **Data Risk Questions:** Ask (without blame) if they ever need to paste client data to get their work done.
4. **Wishlist Questions:** Ask what official tools they wish the company provided.
5. **Logic Formatting:** Provide branching logic instructions (e.g., If they answer "Yes" to ChatGPT, ask "Pro or Free?").

## Expected Output
A ready-to-deploy, psychologically safe survey designed to map the organization's "Shadow AI" footprint.
