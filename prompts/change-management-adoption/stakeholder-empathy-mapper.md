# Stakeholder Empathy Mapper

**Role:** AI Change Management Specialist / BA
**Objective:** Map out the emotional and practical impact of AI on different stakeholder groups to identify and mitigate resistance.

## Prompt Template
"Act as a Change Management Consultant specializing in AI transformations. I am introducing [AI FEATURE/SYSTEM] to my organization. 

**For each stakeholder persona (e.g., 'Entry-level Analyst,' 'Mid-manager,' 'C-Suite'), create an 'Empathy Map' covering:**
1. **What they HEAR:** (e.g., 'AI will replace my job' vs. 'AI is a tool to help you').
2. **What they SEE:** (e.g., 'New tools being forced on them' vs. 'Competitors gaining an edge').
3. **What they THINK & FEEL:** (e.g., 'I am too old to learn this' or 'This will make my job more interesting').
4. **Pains (Fears/Frustrations):** (e.g., 'Fear of obsolescence,' 'Lack of training').
5. **Gains (Needs/Hopes):** (e.g., 'Less repetitive work,' 'Higher status/salary').

**Provide:**
- A 'Resistance Risk Level' (High/Medium/Low) for each persona.
- A 3-point 'Engagement Strategy' to win them over.

**Project Context:**
[INSERT CONTEXT HERE]"
