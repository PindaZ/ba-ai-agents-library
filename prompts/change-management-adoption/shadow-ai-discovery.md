# Shadow AI Discovery

**Role:** AI Business Advisor / IT Auditor
**Objective:** Identify 'Shadow AI' (unofficial AI tools being used by employees) and bring them under official governance.

## Prompt Template
"Act as an IT Governance Specialist. I am conducting a 'Shadow AI Discovery' audit. 

**Help me design an 'AI Discovery Survey' for employees to identify unofficial AI usage in:**
1. **Productivity:** (e.g., 'Do you use ChatGPT/Claude for drafting emails or summaries?').
2. **Coding:** (e.g., 'Are you using GitHub Copilot or Cursor without official approval?').
3. **Creative/Marketing:** (e.g., 'Do you use Midjourney or Canva AI for company assets?').
4. **Data Analysis:** (e.g., 'Are you uploading company data to public AI tools for analysis?').

**Provide:**
- A 10-question survey with 'Anonymous' and 'Confidential' options.
- A 'Risk Assessment Matrix' for each category of shadow AI.
- A 'Transition Plan' for moving users from 'Shadow' to 'Official/Secure' company alternatives.

**Company Context:**
[INSERT COMPANY CONTEXT HERE]"
