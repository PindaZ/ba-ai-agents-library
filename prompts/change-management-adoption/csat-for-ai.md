# CSAT for AI

**Role:** AI Business Analyst / Product Manager
**Objective:** Measure and analyze Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) specifically for AI-driven features and interactions.

## Prompt Template
"Act as a Customer Experience (CX) Analyst. I need to measure the CSAT for our [AI FEATURE NAME]. 

**Develop a 'Post-Interaction Survey' including:**
1. **The 'Big Question':** (e.g., 'On a scale of 1-5, how helpful was the AI's response?').
2. **The 'Accuracy Question':** (e.g., 'Did the AI provide the correct information?').
3. **The 'Effort Question':** (e.g., 'How much effort did you have to put in to get a good result?').
4. **The 'Trust Question':** (e.g., 'How much do you trust the AI's output in this context?').
5. **Qualitative Feedback:** (e.g., 'What could the AI have done better?').

**Provide:**
- A 3-question survey script for the UI.
- A 'Success Dashboard' layout for tracking these metrics.
- Instructions for 'Automated Sentiment Analysis' on the qualitative feedback.

**Feature Description:**
[INSERT FEATURE DESCRIPTION HERE]"
