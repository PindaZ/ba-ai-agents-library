# AI Evangelism Script Writer

**Role:** AI Business Advisor / Change Agent
**Objective:** Create persuasive scripts to "sell" the benefits of AI to different internal departments.

## Prompt Template
"Act as an internal AI Evangelist. I need to pitch our new AI initiative [PROJECT NAME] to the [DEPARTMENT NAME, e.g., 'Legal Team' or 'Marketing Team']. 

**Create a 'Pitch Script' that follows the 'WIIFM' (What's In It For Me) principle:**
1. **The 'Hook':** Acknowledging a current, painful problem the department faces.
2. **The 'Solution':** How the AI directly solves that specific problem.
3. **The 'Benefit':** Focus on 'Human Gains' (e.g., 'Saving you 10 hours of boring work per week') over 'Technical Specs'.
4. **The 'Reassurance':** Addressing the department's #1 most likely fear (e.g., 'Legal risk,' 'Loss of control').
5. **The 'Call to Action':** What is the one thing we need from them today?
