# Value Realization Tracker

**Role:** AI Business Advisor / Financial Analyst
**Objective:** Conduct a 'Post-Implementation Audit' to measure the actual business impact and ROI of an AI initiative 6-12 months after launch.

## Prompt Template
"Act as a Strategic Value Realization Specialist. We launched [AI PROJECT NAME] 12 months ago. 

**Perform a 'Post-Launch Audit' comparing 'Planned Value' vs. 'Actual Value' across:**
1. **Financial Impact:** (e.g., 'Estimated ROI' vs. 'Actual Cost Savings/Revenue Growth').
2. **Operational Efficiency:** (e.g., 'Estimated Time Saved' vs. 'Actual Reduction in Work Hours').
3. **Model Performance:** (e.g., 'Pilot Accuracy' vs. 'Production Accuracy at scale').
4. **User Adoption:** (e.g., 'Target Users' vs. 'Daily Active Users (DAU)').
5. **Unintended Benefits/Consequences:** (e.g., 'New insights discovered' vs. 'Unexpected support costs').

**Conclusion:** Provide a 'Value Realization Scorecard' (1-10) and 3 specific 'Optimization Recommendations' for Year 2.

**Project Context:**
[INSERT PROJECT CONTEXT HERE]"
