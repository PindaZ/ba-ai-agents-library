# AI Training Roadmap Architect

**Role:** AI Business Advisor / L&D Specialist
**Objective:** Design a multi-level training plan to upskill employees for an AI-integrated workplace.

## Prompt Template
"Act as a Corporate Learning & Development (L&D) Architect. We are deploying [AI TOOL NAME] and need a 6-month training roadmap for our employees. 

**Divide the roadmap into 3 Learning Tracks:**
1. **Track 1: AI Literacy (Everyone):** Basics of LLMs, data privacy, and ethical usage.
2. **Track 2: Power Users (BA/Ops):** Advanced prompting, RAG workflows, and auditing AI outputs.
3. **Track 3: Technical Leads (Dev/IT):** Model monitoring, API integration, and performance tuning.

**For each track, provide:**
- 3 core Learning Objectives.
- Recommended Training Format (e.g., 'Workshop,' 'Self-paced,' 'Mentorship').
- Key 'Success Metrics' (How do we know they learned it?).

**Organizational Context:**
[INSERT CONTEXT HERE]"
