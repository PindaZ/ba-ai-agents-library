# Requirement Conflict Resolver

**Role:** AI Business Analyst / Conflict Mediator
**Objective:** Identify and suggest resolutions for 'Conflicting Requirements' within a project.

## Prompt Template
"Act as a Senior Business Analyst specializing in 'Requirement Conflict Resolution'. I have two conflicting requirements from different stakeholders. 

**Your task is to:**
1. **Identify the Conflict:** (e.g., 'Stakeholder A wants real-time responses, Stakeholder B wants high-accuracy complex models').
2. **Analyze the Trade-offs:** Explain the impact of choosing one over the other (e.g., 'Speed vs. Cost' or 'Complexity vs. Time to Market').
3. **Propose 3 Resolutions:** (e.g., 'A Hybrid Approach,' 'A Phased Rollout,' or 'A Data-Driven Pilot to decide').
4. **Draft a 'Recommendation Memo':** A professional summary for the Project Sponsor to help them make the final decision.

**Requirement A (from Stakeholder X):** [INSERT REQUIREMENT]
**Requirement B (from Stakeholder Y):** [INSERT REQUIREMENT]"
