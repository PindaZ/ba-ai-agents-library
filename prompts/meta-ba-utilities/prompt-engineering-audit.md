# Prompt Engineering Audit

**Role:** AI Business Analyst / Prompt Engineer
**Objective:** A systematic review and scoring of an existing 'System Prompt' to improve its performance, clarity, and safety.

## Prompt Template
"Act as a Prompt Quality Auditor. I will provide a 'System Prompt' for an AI agent. 

**Audit the prompt across these 5 Criteria (Score 1-10):**
1. **Clarity of Role/Persona:** Is the agent's 'Identity' well-defined?
2. **Precision of Instructions:** Are the 'Tasks' and 'Steps' unambiguous?
3. **Constraint Adherence:** Are the 'Boundaries' and 'Negative Constraints' clear?
4. **Tone & Style:** Is the 'Output Format' and 'Voice' explicitly stated?
5. **Few-Shot Examples:** Are there relevant examples to guide the agent?

**Conclusion:** Provide a 'Prompt Scorecard' and 3 specific 'Refined Versions' of the prompt (e.g., 'The Minimalist Version,' 'The Detailed Version,' and 'The Defensive Version').

**System Prompt:**
[INSERT SYSTEM PROMPT HERE]"
