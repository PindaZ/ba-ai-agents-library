# Interview Script Generator

**Role:** Senior Business Analyst
**Objective:** Automatically generate a structured 'Interview Script' to elicit requirements from a specific stakeholder persona.

## Prompt Template
"Act as a Senior Business Analyst. I am preparing for a 'Requirement Elicitation Interview' with [STAKEHOLDER PERSONA, e.g., 'Head of Customer Support']. 

**The goal of the interview is to: [INSERT GOAL, e.g., 'Understand the current ticket routing process'].**

**Develop a 30-minute 'Interview Script' including:**
1. **The Introduction:** Explaining the project and why their input is critical.
2. **Current State (As-Is):** 5 specific questions to understand their current workflow and pain points.
3. **Future State (To-Be):** 5 specific questions to identify their 'Ideal AI Solution' and success criteria.
4. **Constraints & Risks:** 3 questions to uncover potential 'Deal-Breakers' or technical limitations.
5. **The Closing:** Summarizing next steps and asking 'What did I miss?'.

**Project Context:**
[INSERT PROJECT CONTEXT HERE]"
