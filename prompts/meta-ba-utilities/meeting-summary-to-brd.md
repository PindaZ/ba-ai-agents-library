# Meeting Summary to BRD

**Role:** AI Business Analyst
**Objective:** Convert a raw meeting transcript or rough notes into a formal Business Requirements Document (BRD) draft.

## Prompt Template
"Act as an AI Requirements Engineer. I will provide a 'Meeting Transcript' from a requirements workshop. 

**Your task is to draft a 'Mini-BRD' following this structure:**
1. **Executive Summary:** A 1-paragraph overview of the project.
2. **Business Objectives:** List the 3-5 primary goals identified in the meeting.
3. **Functional Requirements:** (e.g., 'The system must [Action]').
4. **Non-Functional Requirements:** (e.g., 'The system should be [Quality]').
5. **Assumptions & Constraints:** What was mentioned as a dependency or a limitation?
6. **Open Questions:** List any items that were unresolved during the meeting.

**Meeting Transcript:**
[INSERT TRANSCRIPT HERE]"
