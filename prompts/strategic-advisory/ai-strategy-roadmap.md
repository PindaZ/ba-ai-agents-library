**Objective:** High-level strategic roadmap for AI adoption across the entire enterprise.

## Prompt Template
"Act as a C-Suite AI Advisor. I need a high-level Strategic Roadmap for our company's AI transformation.

**Please include:**
1. **Executive Summary:** A 1-paragraph vision for our AI-first future.
2. **Strategic Pillars:** 3-5 core themes (e.g., 'Customer Experience,' 'Operational Efficiency').
3. **High-Level Timeline:** 1, 3, and 5-year outlooks.
4. **Budgetary Estimates:** Percentage of total IT budget recommended for AI initiatives.
5. **Governance & Ethics:** A brief statement on how we will manage AI risks.
