# Build vs. Buy Decision Matrix

**Role:** AI Business Advisor / Product Manager
**Objective:** Systematically decide whether to develop a custom AI solution or purchase a vendor product.

## Prompt Template
"Act as an AI Product Strategist. I need to decide between 'Building' a custom AI solution or 'Buying' an off-the-shelf product. 

**Analyze the situation across these 5 Criteria:**
1. **Strategic Value:** Is this a core 'Moat' (Build) or a standard 'Efficiency' (Buy)?
2. **Speed to Market:** How much faster is the vendor solution vs. internal dev?
3. **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO):** Licensing fees vs. internal dev and maintenance costs.
4. **Data Privacy & Control:** How sensitive is the data? Can we trust a third-party vendor?
5. **Customization & Flexibility:** Do we have unique requirements that vendors cannot meet?

**Please provide:**
- A weighted scorecard (Build Score vs. Buy Score).
- A list of 'Deal-Breakers' for both options.
- A final recommendation with a 3-year TCO comparison.

**Business Context:**
[INSERT BUSINESS CONTEXT HERE]"
