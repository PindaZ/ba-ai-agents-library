# AI Maturity Model Scorer

**Role:** AI Business Advisor
**Objective:** Benchmarking an organization's AI maturity across five key dimensions.

## Prompt Template
"Act as an AI Maturity Auditor. I will provide a description of my company's current state regarding data, talent, and technology. 

Evaluate our organization on a scale of 1-5 (1: Exploring, 2: Experimenting, 3: Operational, 4: Systemic, 5: AI-First) across these **5 Dimensions**:
1. **Strategy & Governance:** Clear AI leadership and policies.
2. **Data Infrastructure:** Data quality, accessibility, and pipelines.
3. **Talent & Culture:** AI literacy, internal skills, and willingness to change.
4. **Technology & Architecture:** Tooling, models, and integration capabilities.
5. **Business Impact:** Measurable ROI and widespread use cases.

**For each dimension, provide:**
- Current score (1-5).
- 2-3 specific 'Next Steps' to move to the next level.
- Common pitfalls to avoid at this stage.

**Company Profile:**
[INSERT COMPANY PROFILE HERE]"
