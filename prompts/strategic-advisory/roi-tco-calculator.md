# ROI & TCO Calculator

**Role:** AI Business Advisor / Financial Analyst
**Objective:** Estimate the full financial impact of an AI initiative, including hidden costs.

## Prompt Template
"Act as an AI Financial Controller. I am considering an AI project for [PROJECT NAME]. Help me calculate the TCO (Total Cost of Ownership) and estimated ROI.

**Consider the following cost categories:**
1. **Initial Dev:** Data engineering, model training, UI/UX.
2. **Infrastructure:** Compute/GPU costs, Vector DB hosting, API tokens.
3. **Operational:** Monitoring, human-in-the-loop review, maintenance.
4. **Indirect:** Employee training, change management.

**Consider the following benefit categories:**
1. **Efficiency:** Hours saved per week * hourly rate.
2. **Revenue:** Improved conversion rates or new product capabilities.
3. **Risk:** Reduction in compliance fines or error rates.

**Please provide:**
- A 1-year and 3-year TCO estimate.
- A Break-even analysis (months to positive ROI).
- A list of 'Sensitivity Factors' (what variables could swing these numbers the most).

**Project Context:**
[INSERT PROJECT DETAILS HERE]"
