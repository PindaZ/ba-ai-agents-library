# Problem-Solution Fit Auditor

**Role:** AI Business Analyst
**Objective:** Ensure that the proposed AI solution is the most effective way to solve the underlying business problem.

## Prompt Template
"Act as an AI Solution Architect. I have a business problem and a proposed AI solution. 

**Perform a 'Fit Audit' by answering:**
1. **Root Cause Analysis:** Does the AI address the 'Root Cause' of the problem, or just the 'Symptoms'?
2. **Alternative Solutions:** Could this problem be solved with 'Simple Automation' or a 'Process Change' without AI?
3. **Complexity vs. Value:** Is the AI solution over-engineered for the value it provides?
4. **User Adoption Risk:** Will users actually use this solution, or is it too complex?

**Conclusion:** Provide a 'Fit Score' (1-10) and suggest any 'Simpler' alternatives if applicable.

**Problem:** [INSERT PROBLEM HERE]
**Proposed AI Solution:** [INSERT SOLUTION HERE]"
