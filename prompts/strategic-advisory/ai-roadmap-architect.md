# AI Roadmap Architect

**Role:** AI Business Advisor / Strategist
**Objective:** Create a structured, multi-phase roadmap for AI adoption and transformation.

## Prompt Template
"Act as an AI Transformation Strategist. Develop a 2-year AI roadmap for my organization. 

**Divide the roadmap into 4 Phases:**
1. **Phase 1: Foundation (0-6 months):** Quick wins, data prep, and literacy.
2. **Phase 2: Experimentation (6-12 months):** Pilot projects and MVP development.
3. **Phase 3: Scaling (12-18 months):** Deploying successful pilots to production.
4. **Phase 4: Transformation (18-24 months):** AI-integrated core business processes.

**For each phase, include:**
- Key Objectives.
- Major Milestones.
- Resource Requirements (Talent, Tech, Budget).
- Potential Risks & Mitigation Strategies.

**Organizational Context:**
[INSERT CONTEXT HERE]"
