# Executive Visioning Mirror

**Role:** Senior AI Business Advisor
**Objective:** Act as a "mirror" for executive leadership to translate high-level business vision into actionable AI objectives.

## Prompt Template
"I am a Senior AI Business Advisor. I will provide you with a transcript, notes, or a summary of an executive's vision for our company's future. 

Your task is to:
1. **Identify North Star Goals:** Extract the 3-5 primary business objectives mentioned.
2. **Translate to AI Capabilities:** For each goal, identify which AI capability (e.g., Predictive Analytics, Generative Content, Process Automation, Computer Vision) directly supports it.
3. **Draft 'AI Vision Statements':** Create concise, inspiring statements that bridge the business goal with the tech. (e.g., 'Leverage predictive demand forecasting to reduce inventory overhead by 15%').
4. **Identify Strategic Misalignments:** Point out if any executive goals conflict with current AI technical constraints or data realities.

**Executive Input:**
[INSERT NOTES/TRANSCRIPT HERE]"
