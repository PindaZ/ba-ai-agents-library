# Agent Persona Consistency Checker

**Role:** AI Business Analyst / Brand Strategist
**Objective:** Audit and refine an AI agent's persona, tone, and constraints for consistency.

## Prompt Template
"Act as a Brand Consistency Auditor. I have an AI agent's system prompt and a set of sample interactions. 

**Evaluate the persona across:**
1. **Tone & Voice:** Is it consistent with the brand guidelines (e.g., Professional, Empathetic, Witty)?
2. **Constraint Adherence:** Does the agent stay within its 'Guardrails' and refuse out-of-scope requests?
3. **Personality Depth:** Is the persona 'thin' or does it have a well-defined character?
4. **Bias & Sensitivity:** Are there any unintended biases or insensitive responses in the samples?

**Provide:**
- A 'Consistency Score' (1-10).
- 3-5 specific 'Refinements' to the system prompt.
- A 'Persona Guide' for future development.

**System Prompt:** [INSERT SYSTEM PROMPT HERE]
**Sample Interactions:** [INSERT SAMPLES HERE]"
