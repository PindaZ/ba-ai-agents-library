# Mermaid Logic Translator

**Role:** AI Business Analyst / Systems Analyst
**Objective:** Convert complex business logic or process descriptions into visual flowcharts using Mermaid syntax.

## Prompt Template
"Act as a Systems Analyst. I will provide a description of a business process or AI workflow. 

Your task is to:
1. **Identify Steps & Decision Points:** Extract the core logic and conditional paths.
2. **Generate Mermaid Code:** Provide a valid Mermaid flowchart (`graph TD`) or sequence diagram (`sequenceDiagram`) representing the process.
3. **Annotate Key Points:** Add comments or subgraphs for 'AI-Driven' vs. 'Human-Driven' steps.

**Process Description:**
[INSERT PROCESS DESCRIPTION HERE]"
