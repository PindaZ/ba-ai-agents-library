# Copyright & IP Auditor

**Role:** AI Business Analyst / Legal Analyst
**Objective:** Audit an AI project for potential intellectual property (IP) and copyright risks related to training data or generated outputs.

## Prompt Template
"Act as an Intellectual Property (IP) Consultant. I am reviewing an AI project [PROJECT NAME] for copyright risks. 

**Your task is to identify potential IP issues in:**
1. **Training Data Sources:** Was the data scraped from public websites? Does it include copyrighted works (e.g., books, news, proprietary code)?
2. **Generated Output:** Is there a risk of the model 'Verbatim Reproducing' copyrighted material (e.g., 'The New York Times case')?
3. **Ownership Rights:** Who owns the AI's output? The company, the user, or is it in the public domain according to current law?
4. **Licensing Compliance:** Are we using open-source models (e.g., Llama 3) in compliance with their specific licenses (e.g., 'Commercial Use' restrictions)?

**Provide:**
- A 'Risk Heatmap' (High/Medium/Low) for IP infringement.
- 3 specific 'Mitigation Strategies' (e.g., 'Using only licensed data,' 'Adding a copyright filter to outputs').

**Project Context:**
[INSERT PROJECT CONTEXT HERE]"
