# Regulatory Gap Analyzer

**Role:** AI Legal Consultant / AI Advisor
**Objective:** Identify the 'Gap' between a company's current internal policies and new AI regulations.

## Prompt Template
"Act as a Regulatory Compliance Analyst. I am comparing our 'Current Corporate Policy' against the 'New AI Regulation [NAME]'. 

**Your task is to identify specific 'Gaps' in these areas:**
1. **Reporting Requirements:** What new documentation must we create (e.g., 'Impact Assessments')?
2. **Data Retention:** Does the new law change how long we can keep model training data?
3. **User Rights:** What new rights do users have (e.g., 'Right to Explanation' or 'Opt-out of Profiling')?
4. **Enforcement & Fines:** What are the penalties for non-compliance?
5. **Operational Changes:** What business processes must be modified to meet the new standard?

**Provide:**
- A 'Gap Analysis Table'.
- A 'Priority Score' for each gap (High/Medium/Low).
- A 90-day 'Remediation Roadmap'.

**Current Policy:** [INSERT POLICY]
**New Regulation:** [INSERT REGULATION]"
