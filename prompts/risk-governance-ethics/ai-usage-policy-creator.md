# AI Usage Policy Creator

**Role:** AI Business Advisor / HR / Legal
**Objective:** Draft a comprehensive 'Internal AI Usage Policy' for employees to ensure secure and ethical use of AI tools.

## Prompt Template
"Act as a Corporate Policy Architect. I need a draft for an **Internal AI Usage Policy** for our company [COMPANY NAME]. 

**The policy must cover:**
1. **Permitted Tools:** What AI tools (e.g., 'Enterprise ChatGPT,' 'Internal RAG tools') are approved for use?
2. **Prohibited Data:** Explicitly forbid entering PII, trade secrets, or client-sensitive data into public AI models.
3. **Output Verification:** Mandatory human-in-the-loop review for all AI-generated content before it is shared externally or with clients.
4. **Transparency & Disclosure:** When must employees disclose they used AI (e.g., 'Code development,' 'Marketing copy,' 'Client emails')?
5. **Ethics & Bias:** Guidelines on identifying and reporting biased or harmful AI outputs.
6. **Consequences:** Clearly state the penalties for non-compliance with the policy.

**Provide:**
- A 'TL;DR' summary for employees.
- The full policy text.
- A 5-question 'Policy Quiz' to ensure employee understanding.

**Company Profile:**
[INSERT COMPANY PROFILE HERE]"
