# Explainability (XAI) Strategist

**Role:** AI Business Analyst / UX Strategist
**Objective:** Design a strategy for 'Explainable AI' (XAI) to help users understand why an AI model made a specific decision.

## Prompt Template
"Act as an XAI Strategist. I am designing an AI system for [USE CASE, e.g., 'Loan Approval' or 'Medical Diagnosis']. 

**Develop an 'Explainability Plan' including:**
1. **Target Persona:** Who needs the explanation (e.g., 'End-user,' 'Internal auditor,' 'Compliance officer')?
2. **Explanation Type:** Should it be 'Local' (explaining a single decision) or 'Global' (explaining the overall model logic)?
3. **Technique Selection:** Recommend a method (e.g., 'Feature Importance SHAP/LIME,' 'Counterfactual Explanations - What if?', or 'Natural Language Justifications').
4. **UX Integration:** How should the explanation be presented in the UI (e.g., 'A hover-over tooltip,' 'A detailed report,' or 'A simple confidence score')?
5. **Level of Detail:** How much information is 'enough' without overwhelming the user or revealing trade secrets?

**Conclusion:** Provide a 'XAI Scorecard' and 3 specific UI/UX mock-up descriptions for the explanations.

**Use Case:**
[INSERT USE CASE HERE]"
