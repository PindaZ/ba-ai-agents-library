# AI Ethics Framework

## 1. Transparency
How is the AI making decisions? Is it explainable?

## 2. Fairness & Bias
How are we auditing for algorithmic bias?

## 3. Privacy & Security
How is user data protected and where is it stored?

## 4. Accountability
Who is responsible for the AI's output and performance?

## 5. Human-in-the-Loop
When and where is human intervention required?
