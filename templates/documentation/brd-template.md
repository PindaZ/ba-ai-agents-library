# Business Requirements Document (BRD) Template

## 1. Executive Summary
Overview of the problem and proposed solution.

## 2. Business Objectives
What are we trying to achieve? (SMART goals).

## 3. Current State Analysis
Existing workflows, systems, and pain points.

## 4. Proposed Solution
High-level overview of the future state.

## 5. Functional Requirements
Detailed list of features and capabilities.

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements
Performance, security, availability, scalability.

## 7. Assumptions & Constraints
Dependencies and limitations.
